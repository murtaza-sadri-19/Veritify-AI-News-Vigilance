About TruthTrack AI

Empowering truth in the digital age with AI-driven fact verification

Our Mission

TruthTrack AI Vigilance combats misinformation by providing users with access to verified fact-checks from reputable sources. We aggregate results from established fact-checking organizations to help you determine the truthfulness of claims with confidence.

How It Works

  1. 1 Process: Analyze your claim using NLP to identify key facts and entities
  2. 2 Query: Search multiple fact-checking APIs and databases
  3. 3 Analyze: Aggregate results from various credible sources
  4. 4 Score: Calculate truthfulness score based on credibility and relevance
  5. 5 Present: Display verified information with links to original sources

Credible Sources

We integrate with established fact-checking organizations, including:

  • Google Fact Check Tools
  • FactCheck.org
  • PolitiFact
  • Snopes
  • Real-time News APIs

Understanding Scores

Our truthfulness scores range from 0-100, with color-coded indicators:

80-100: Claim appears to be mostly true
60-79: Claim contains truth with minor inaccuracies
40-59: Mixed truthfulness, needs verification
20-39: Claim appears to be mostly false
0-19: Claim is disputed or false

Key Features

  • Top-K article ranking for precision results
  • Credibility-aware scoring system
  • Multi-metric relevance analysis (semantic, textual, NLP)
  • Location and genre classification
  • Detailed source attribution and links
  • Real-time news integration
