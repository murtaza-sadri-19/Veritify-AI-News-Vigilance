About Veritify

We're building the future of fact-checking with artificial intelligence and human-centered design

Our Mission

In an era of information overload and misinformation, Veritify exists to empower people with the tools they need to verify facts quickly and accurately. We believe that access to truth should be instant, transparent, and accessible to everyone.

How It Works

Veritify uses advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) and machine learning models to analyze claims against millions of verified sources. Our two-stage verification pipeline combines lexical and semantic analysis with entailment detection to provide accurate, nuanced verdicts.

Our Approach

Unlike simple keyword matching, our AI understands context, sentiment, and factual relationships. We cross-reference claims against trusted news outlets, fact-checking organizations, and academic sources to provide you with comprehensive evidence.

Meet the Team

Veritify was built by Aditya Desai and Murtaza Khan, passionate engineers dedicated to combating misinformation through technology. With expertise in machine learning, natural language processing, and full-stack development, we're committed to creating tools that matter.

Transparency & Trust

We believe fact-checking should be transparent. That's why we always show you the sources behind our verdicts, the evidence we found, and how confident we are in our analysis. You can verify our work and make informed decisions.
