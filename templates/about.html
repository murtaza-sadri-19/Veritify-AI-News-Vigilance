About TruthTrack AI Vigilance

Our Mission

TruthTrack AI Vigilance aims to combat misinformation by providing users with access to verified fact-checks from reputable sources. We aggregate results from established fact-checking organizations to help you determine the truthfulness of claims.

How It Works

When you submit a claim, our system:

  1. Processes your claim to identify key facts and entities
  2. Queries multiple fact-checking APIs and databases
  3. Analyzes and aggregates the results from various sources
  4. Calculates a truthfulness score based on available fact-checks
  5. Presents you with verified information and links to original sources

Our Sources

We integrate with established fact-checking organizations, including:

  • Google Fact Check Tools
  • FactCheck.org
  • PolitiFact
  • Snopes
  • Other reputable fact-checking sources

Understanding Scores

Our truthfulness scores range from 0-100:

  • 80-100: Claim appears to be mostly true
  • 60-79: Claim contains some truth but may have inaccuracies
  • 40-59: Claim has mixed truthfulness
  • 20-39: Claim appears to be mostly false
  • 0-19: Claim is disputed or labeled as false
